How do you change or reset your password?

Change your password

To change your password on NursingonDemand app if you're already loggged in:

1. Click Settings in the top right corner of home

2. Click Update Password

3. Edit Current Password and New Password what you want to change with

4. Click Save Changes.

If you're logged in but have forgotten your password, follow the steps under Change your Password then click Forgot your password?
