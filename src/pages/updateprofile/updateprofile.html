How can i update my profile?

Update My Profile

To Update My Profile on Nursing on Demand if you're already loggged in:

1. Click Sidemenu in the top right corner of home

2. Click Setting in the bottom corner of Sidemenu

3. Click My Profile in the top of Settings

4. Click Profile pic

5. Edit changes

5. Save the changes
